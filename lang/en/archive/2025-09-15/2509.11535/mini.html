A hybrid quantum algorithm finds better solutions for complex problems faster than classical counterparts.
1

A regular computer searches for the energy minimum like a marble rolling into a dip and can get stuck in a puddle. A quantum processor with 100 qubits works differently: it probes all valleys at once, like water instantly finding the deepest drain. This brings closer the era of quantum supremacy in everyday tasks—from scheduling to logistics.

🎯 The Ising model, created a century ago to describe magnets, now optimizes cargo loading and even analyzes social networks.
