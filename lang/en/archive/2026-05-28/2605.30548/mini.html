The Swirling Dance of Neutron Stars ⚡ экспресс

Original: "Magnetic Eruption and Nucleosynthesis in GR{\nu}MHD Simulations of Spinning Neutron Star Mergers"
· Allen Wen, Jay V. Kalinani, Michail Chabanov, Manuela Campanelli, Riccardo Ciolfi, Yosef Zlochower
arXiv:2605.30548 · 2026-05-28 · CC BY · ⏱ 1 min · High Energy General Relativity
The spin of neutron stars in their deadly dance determines whether a narrow beam or a cloud of debris appears.
Links in the knowledge graph 1

Spinning neutron stars collide differently: aligned spins produce a cosmic blowtorch, while opposite spins give a messy firework. In these crucibles, radioactive nickel-56 is forged, its light potentially revealing the birth of the heaviest elements.

🎯 In a few seconds, such a merger produces more gold than humans have mined in all of history.

🎬 In Peter Hamilton’s novel *The Neutronium Alchemist*, neutron star matter is the raw material for fantastic elements. Nature truly practices alchemy: in these mergers, the r-process forges gold and uranium.
