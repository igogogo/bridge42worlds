Original: "Parity-violating Dark Photon Halos"
Vibrations in an invisible field spawn swirling particles of dark light, driving clouds of dark matter into supermassive black holes at breakneck speed.

Invisible axion fields in the early universe spawned vortices of dark photons. These cosmic blenders helped dark matter clouds collapse directly into giant black holes. Thus, tiny quantum tremors could have become the seeds of the most massive monsters puzzling astronomers.

🎯 Axions may be a hundred trillion trillion times lighter than an electron, but their collective trembling creates gravitational funnels the size of a star cluster.
