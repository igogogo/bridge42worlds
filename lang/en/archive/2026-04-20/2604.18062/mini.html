Original: "Towards a Foundation-Model Paradigm for Aerodynamic Prediction in Three-dimensional Design"
After studying thousands of wing shapes, AI predicts their flight behavior almost flawlessly — even on sparse data.

Previously, training an accurate aerodynamic model was costly. Now the approach is different: first, AI sees 30,000 different wings, then fine-tunes for a specific task. Error is only 0.36% on 450 examples. A rapid design tool is already online.

🎯 The model is already working on the WebWing website: move the wing with your mouse and watch lift in real time.
