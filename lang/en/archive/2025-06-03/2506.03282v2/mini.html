Original: "Coherence and Entanglement in a Non-commutative Spacetime"
Researchers found that even in warped spacetime, quantum links can form and survive—keeping particles in unbreakable unity.

Even in open quantum spacetime, where everything should blur, particles manage to stay in sync. The reason lies in the deformation of energy and momentum addition laws, acting like an invisible conductor.

🎯 If decoherence didn't destroy quantum effects, a single observation could rewrite reality, and our classical world would never have emerged.
