Dark Forces Twisted the Light from the Past express

Original: "Cosmic Birefringence from the Atacama Cosmology Telescope Data Release 6"
· P. Diego-Palazuelos, E. Komatsu
arXiv:2509.13654 · 2025-09-17 · CC BY 4.0 · 1 min
Cosmology
The oldest light in the universe reached us slightly twisted — possibly rotated by a dark matter field.
1

The oldest light in the universe reached us slightly twisted — as if an invisible field had spun it. Astronomers suspect dark matter. If confirmed, we’ll see traces of what most of the cosmos is made of for the first time.

🎯 A 0.2-degree twist is like walking from Earth to the Moon and deviating from a straight line by just the thickness of a human hair.
