Scientists have developed a quantum computer scheme that corrects errors on its own without external help.

Quantum computers have acquired built-in immunity to errors. The new two-dimensional scheme dampens noise by itself, like skin heals a wound. This is a path to eternal quantum computation and breakthroughs in encryption and chemistry.

🎯 A single cosmic ray particle can corrupt data in an unprotected quantum computer—defending against such pervasive noise requires sophisticated tricks.
