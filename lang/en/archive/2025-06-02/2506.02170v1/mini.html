Original: "Quantum Vacuum in Matter"
Inside special cavities, the invisible fabric of vacuum reshapes the structure of materials.

Emptiness is not empty at all. In every cubic centimeter of space, particles of light are constantly born and vanish. Scientists have learned to use this hidden energy to change the properties of materials without touching them. This opens the way to technology that runs literally on 'nothing'.

🎯 [scientist:Richard Feynman]Feynman[/scientist] calculated that in the volume of a teacup, there is enough vacuum energy to boil all the oceans. Scientists are now seeking ways to tame this power.

🎬 From 'Stargate' with their zero-point modules to Asimov's novels, sci-fi writers have long dreamed of conquering vacuum energy.
