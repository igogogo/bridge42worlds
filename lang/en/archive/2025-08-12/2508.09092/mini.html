The Jiuzhang 4.0 quantum processor handles in microseconds a task that would take the most powerful supercomputer 10^42 years.

The Chinese quantum computer Jiuzhang 4.0 solved in 25 microseconds a problem that would stump any supercomputer for longer than the universe has existed. The machine operates with light, pushing photons through an intricate maze, and captures a unique pattern of their arrival. Such a breakthrough promises new drugs and unbreakable codes.

🎯 If the El Capitan supercomputer had started crunching numbers at the Big Bang, by now it wouldn't have completed even a fraction of what Jiuzhang 4.0 does in 25 microseconds.
