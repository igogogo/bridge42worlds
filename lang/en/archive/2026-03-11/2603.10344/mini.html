Original: "Machine learning the arrow of time in solid-state spins"
Scientists trained a neural network to determine the direction of time from quantum processor data.

A diamond quantum processor helped a neural network see the arrow of time. Heat flows on ten qubits and machine learning recognize past and future with 92% accuracy. A step toward quantum engines and a timeless understanding of irreversibility.

🎯 Remove the measurements from the process, and symmetry returned — the neural network could no longer tell the direction of time. It is quantum measurements that 'switch on' irreversibility.

🎬 The idea of recognizing the arrow of time in quantum data harks back to the film 'Tenet', where inverted entropy makes time flow backwards.
