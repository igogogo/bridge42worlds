Original: "Magnetic levitation and spatial superposition of a nanodiamond with a current-carrying chip"
Physicists have devised a way to make a tiny diamond levitate and be in two places at once—to test whether gravity itself is quantum.

A tiny levitating diamond can be in two places at once! Physicists have devised a tabletop experiment: using magnetic fields, the particle hovers and splits. If two such particles entangle via gravity, it would prove the quantum nature of attraction. Perhaps soon we’ll understand how space-time is woven.

🎯 The state of dual existence lasts less than an instant—a tenth of a second—but that’s long enough for measurements.
