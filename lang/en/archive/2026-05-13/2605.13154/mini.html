Three scientists offer different explanations for why quantum particles behave as if they break the laws of logic and speed.

Quantum particles act as if they instantly 'agree' at any distance. Three physicists propose solutions: abandoning reality, the limitations of observers, or extra dimensions. This changes the very foundation of how we understand the world.

🎯 The strength of quantum connections in our three-dimensional space is limited (Tsirelson's bound). In worlds with a different number of dimensions, correlations could be much stronger.

🎬 Instantaneous particle connection is the physical analogue of the ansible from science fiction, a device for communication at any distance, described in Ursula Le Guin's novels.
