Gravity Born from Disorder: Physicists Find a Universal Recipe ⚡ экспресс

Original: "Entropic route to Brown-York tensor: A unified framework for null and timelike hypersurfaces"
· Krishnakanta Bhattacharya, Bhera Ram, Bibhas Ranjan Majhi
arXiv:2605.22434 · 2026-05-21 · CC BY · ⏱ 1 min · General Relativity HEP Theory
One entropy recipe yields both Einstein's equations and a way to compute energy at boundaries.
Links in the knowledge graph 1

Gravity isn’t fundamental—it’s like heat emerging from chaos. Physicists derived Einstein’s equations from a simple law of entropy. This explains why black holes 'glow' with information and how spacetime responds to matter like a pond’s surface to a stone.

🎯 A black hole stores information about its disorder on its surface—as if all data is written on the shell, not inside.
