The Universe's Secret Phase: How the Dark Sector Reconciled the Astronomers ⚡ экспресс

Original: "Hot New Early Dark Energy: Dark Radiation Matter Decoupling"
· Mathias Garny, Florian Niedermann, Henrique Rubira, Martin S. Sloth
arXiv:2508.03795 · 2025-08-05 · CC BY 4.0 · ⏱ 1 min · Cosmology HEP Phenomenology HEP Theory
An early phase transition in the dark sector explains the mystery of the universe's expansion rate.
Links in the knowledge graph 1

The cosmos is expanding faster than predicted. Scientists have proposed that in the universe’s infancy, something like a switch flicked—a sudden phase transition in the invisible dark sector. It gave expansion a boost and reconciled the star-based maps with the cosmic microwave background.

🎯 Phase transitions aren’t fiction: it’s believed that in the first second after the Big Bang, the universe underwent several such transformations, giving rise to the forces of nature we know.
