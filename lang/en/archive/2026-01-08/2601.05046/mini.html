The Mpemba effect enabled the creation of ultrafast quantum thermometers.

Hot water sometimes freezes faster than cold—this paradox is known as the Mpemba effect. Now quantum physicists have turned it into a working tool: starting from a hot state, nanoprobes measure temperature more accurately and quickly than any equilibrium methods.

🎯 The Mpemba effect remains a mystery: scientists cannot unequivocally explain it. Hypotheses include evaporation, convection, and supercooling.
