Cohomology

📊 0 articles
If homology counts holes, then cohomology is a way to measure how functions on a space can 'twist' around these holes.
💡 Cohomology allows multiplication of classes — this gives a ring structure that homology lacks.
Cohomology is the dual concept to homology: instead of chains, cochains (functions on simplices) are considered, and the boundary operator is replaced by the coboundary. Cohomology groups H^n(X) are the quotient of cocycles by coboundaries. They form a ring with multiplication (cup product).

History

Cohomology appeared in the 1930s in the works of James Alexander, André Weil, and Hassler Whitney.

How it works

For a space, a cochain complex is built: groups C^n(X) are functions from n-dimensional simplices to an abelian group. The coboundary operator δ: C^n → C^{n+1} is defined dually to the boundary. Cocycles are cochains with δ=0, coboundaries are images of δ. Cohomology is cocycles modulo coboundaries.

💡 De Rham cohomology is a variant of cohomology using differential forms, and it is isomorphic to singular cohomology for smooth manifolds.
Cohomology is a contravariant functor from the category of topological spaces to the category of graded rings. It is dual to homology: the cochain complex C^n(X; G) = Hom(C_n(X), G), where C_n are singular chains. The coboundary δ = ∂^T. Cohomology H^n(X; G) = Ker δ_n / Im δ_{n-1}. The cup product ∪: H^p × H^q → H^{p+q} turns the direct sum H^*(X) into a graded ring. Cohomology gives finer invariants than homology.

History

Cohomology was introduced in the 1930s. James Alexander and André Weil constructed cohomology for simplicial complexes. Hassler Whitney gave an axiomatic definition. In the 1940s, Samuel Eilenberg and Norman Steenrod developed the Steenrod-Eilenberg axioms for homology and cohomology. Later, sheaf cohomology (Grothendieck) and étale cohomology appeared.

How it works

The cochain complex is built dually to the chain complex. Cocycles are cochains that vanish on boundaries. Coboundaries are cochains that are coboundaries of others. Cohomology is equivalence classes of cocycles modulo coboundaries. The cup product is induced by the diagonal map of the space. Cohomology is computed using spectral sequences and obstruction theory.

Open problems & fun facts

Hodge conjecture on the representation of cohomology of complex projective varieties; Computing cohomology for spaces with singularities

💡 De Rham cohomology is a variant of cohomology using differential forms, and it is isomorphic to singular cohomology for smooth manifolds.
