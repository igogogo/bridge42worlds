The Hubble Space Telescope—a legendary observatory operating since 1990. Like a window to the Universe, free from atmospheric distortions, giving us the most beautiful and deepest images of space.

History

Launched in 1990, but due to a mirror defect, the first images were blurry. In 1993, a shuttle mission corrected the optics. Hubble has operated for over 30 years, making many discoveries.

How it works

The telescope with a 2.4-meter mirror collects light in optical and ultraviolet ranges. It is in low Earth orbit. It measured the acceleration of the Universe's expansion, discovered exoplanets, and obtained deep fields.
