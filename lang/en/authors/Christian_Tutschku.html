Quantum Copying: Encryption Beats the Ban

The no-cloning theorem says a qubit can't be copied. But physicists found a loophole — encrypted cloning with a one-time key. An experiment on IBM's 154-qubit processor proved the method works under real noise. The real ban is not on copying, but on reading an extra copy.
