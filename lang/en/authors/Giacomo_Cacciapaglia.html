When a Black Hole Can Split Apart

The laws of physics forbid black holes from dividing: their total area cannot decrease, just like entropy. But rapid rotation changes the rules, allowing tiny fragments to break off. In worlds with extra dimensions, fragmentation is even easier, revealing secrets of primordial black holes.
