Two massive black holes swept clean the center of a galaxy

Telescope images revealed a region in a distant galaxy where billions of stars had disappeared. The void was caused by a pair of supermassive black holes whose combined mass is an absolute record. The discovery helps understand the growth of giant galaxies.
