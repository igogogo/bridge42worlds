Cosmic Necklace Reveals Secrets of Galaxy Birth

Astronomers peered into the early Universe and saw how three massive galaxies, merging, eject gas. This gas compresses into clumps resembling necklace beads and glows brightly. These cosmic 'jewelry' pieces are an important stage in the construction of the most massive galaxies.
