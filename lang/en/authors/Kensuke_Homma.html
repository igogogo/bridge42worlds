Earth as a Lens for Searching Dark Matter

By turning Earth into a gravitational lens, scientists found a way to focus dark matter particles into a single point. There, a laser makes them instantly convert into light, which is then caught by detectors. The method promises to detect even ultralight particles from thousands of light-years away
