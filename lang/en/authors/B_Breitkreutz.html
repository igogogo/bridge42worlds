A Tiny Skew in the Atomic Nucleus and the Mystery of the Universe

Inside a giant magnetic ring, nuclei were accelerated and their spin axis was monitored. If there were a charge imbalance inside, the axis would tilt. No tilt was found, but a new precise limit was set. This is key to the puzzle: why after the Big Bang, matter survived over antimatter.
