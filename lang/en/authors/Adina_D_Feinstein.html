Watery Surprise of a Young Super-Earth

For the first time, the JWST telescope studied the atmosphere of the young super-Earth V1298 Tau c. Water vapor was detected, but there were fewer heavy element 'spices' than in mature planets. This suggests that planets accumulate metals over time—like a dish that gets saltier as it cooks.
