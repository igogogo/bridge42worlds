The 'Chef' Black Hole That Prevents Star Birth

The model shows: the chef black hole heats galactic gas, preventing stars from igniting, and ejects metal-rich material while pulling in fresh gas. This explains the near-total lack of heavy elements in galaxy Abell 2744-QSO1 and the unusual ratio of the hole's mass to stars.
