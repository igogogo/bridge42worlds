Hot Cools Faster: The Quantum Mpemba Effect

Contrary to intuition, an overheated quantum particle reaches equilibrium with its environment faster than a lukewarm one. The Mpemba effect was tested on a single atom and integrated into a micro-fridge, boosting its cooling power. This promises advances in cooling quantum computers.
