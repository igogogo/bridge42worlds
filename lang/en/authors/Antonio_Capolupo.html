Ghosts of the Universe: Neutrinos as Dark Matter

Scientists propose a new take on dark matter: it might be made of ordinary neutrinos. Their quantum transformations in curved space create extra gravity, like ghostly glue. This holds stars at the edges of galaxies without needing new particles.
