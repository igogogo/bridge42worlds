Superfluid Helium Feels How the Earth Spins

Earth drags the fabric of space-time along with it — an effect predicted by Einstein. Scientists propose catching it with superfluid helium and a highly precise detector. The device will notice rotation slower than one revolution in the entire history of Earth.
