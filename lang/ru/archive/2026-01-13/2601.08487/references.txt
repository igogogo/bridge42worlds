B. Computational details
The DIRAC program package (githash:
d764921d6) [ S17, S18] is used for all electronic structure
calculations otherwise explicitly described. We employ
the three kinds of relativistic Hamiltonians: Dirac-
Coulomb (DC), Dirac-Coulomb-Gaunt (DCG), or the
exact two-component molecular-mean ﬁeld (X2Cmmf)
Hamiltonian [
S19] based on the DC Hamiltonian
(2DCM ). We employ the dyall.v3z and dyall.v4z basis
sets [ S20] with tight exponents obtained in Ref. [ S1]
(v3z+3s2p, v4z+3s2p) for the calculations using the
DIRAC program.
Our calculations can be categorized into the baseline
and corrections. Potentially important corrections for
these states are identiﬁed, referring to the uncertainty
estimation in our previous study [
S1], and these correc-
tions are then added to the baseline. For the basis-set
correction and core-valence correlations, theXi calcula-

and the uncalculated mass regions are interpolated using
a linear function.
