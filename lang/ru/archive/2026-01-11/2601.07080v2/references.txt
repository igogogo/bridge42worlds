D.MANTLE PHASES
We first structure the phase diagram by adopting pa-
rameterized cubic spline radial functions for the solidus,
Tsld(r), the liquidus,T liq(r), and the temperature pro-
fileT crit(r) corresponding to a critical melt fraction

Miyazaki & Korenaga 2019). For temperatures above

fines the rheological transition from a deformable solid
matrix that is penetrated by a network of percolating

around which a regime of mushy magma exists (e.g. Abe
1997).
