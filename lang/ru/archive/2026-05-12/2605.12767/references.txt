octupole-deformed nuclei, χEFT provides the bridge from hadronic CPV operators to nuclear forces
that generate Schiff moments. χEFT is built on the approximate chiral symmetry of QCD, which is
spontaneously broken by non-perturbative color confinement and explicitly broken by quark masses and
electroweak interactions. Below the chiral symmetry-breaking scale Λχ ≃1.2 GeV , pions emerge as the
relevant low-energy degrees of freedom and set the power-counting scale. At energies belowΛχ , chiral
symmetry is both spontaneously broken by non-perturbative color confinement23, 155 and explicitly broken
by the quark masses and electroweak interactions. Chiral symmetry breaking produces pions that set the
IR energy scale for power counting.

chromo-EDM, and CPV four-quark interactions8, 15, 16, 51, 156. These Wilson coefficients are run to low
energies and matched onto χEFT LECs that parameterize the nuclear CPV potential HCPV . Table 1 in the
