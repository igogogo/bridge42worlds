S2 Two-photon path alternatives and derivation of interferom-

When a two-photon state𝜌(2)

(OAM) detector shown in Fig. 4(b) of the main text, three scenarios are possible: (i) both photons

counts at one of the single-photon counting modules (SPCMs). Consequently, our analysis of
coincidence detection focuses exclusively on the third scenario.
