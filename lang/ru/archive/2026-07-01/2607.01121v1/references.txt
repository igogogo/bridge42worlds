Refs. 51, 54 find that hierarchical mergers evolve faster than the first-generation mergers with

The impact of the variance threshold for likelihood estimation
Monte Carlo uncertainty in the likelihood evaluation may influence the hierarchical inference
55. To check whether such uncertainty has impact on our results, we repeat our analysis with a
more permissive cutoffV arthr = 4, and compare to the results in the main text, i.e., inferred with
V arthr = 1. It turns out that the mass distributions are well consistent between the two results (see
Supplementary Figure 6), demonstrating that the main finding of this work is reliable.
