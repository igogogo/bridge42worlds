Procedure

employed an Error-in-Variables (EIV) regression (Fuller 1987;
Tremaine et al. 2002; Kelly 2007). This method allowed us to
evaluate the velocity difference∆Vat the exact location of every
gas data point while properly propagating uncertainties in both
velocity and galactocentric radius.40
