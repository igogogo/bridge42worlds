Stars Are Not Made from a Single Mold: The Gaia Discovery

The Gaia telescope studied stellar nurseries and discovered that the ratio of giant stars to dwarfs differs from one gas-and-dust cloud to another. It depends on the density and temperature of the ‘crib’, meaning there is no single cosmic recipe for star formation.
