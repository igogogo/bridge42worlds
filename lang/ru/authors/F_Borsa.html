Mirror Planet: Reflected Light of an Exo-Neptune

Using the ESPRESSO spectrograph on the VLT, scientists have reliably detected reflected light from the ultra-hot exo-Neptune LTT-9779 b. The planet is incredibly bright—its geometric albedo is 0.88, making it similar to Venus but in miniature.
