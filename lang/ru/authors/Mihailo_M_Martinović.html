Mixer Waves of the Solar Wind: How the Corona Heats Up

Near the Sun, left-handed mixer waves rage, making the energy spectrum steeper. Right-handed waves are quieter close by but intensify farther out thanks to hot proton streams. This discovery helps predict space weather and understand why the corona is hundreds of times hotter than the surface.
