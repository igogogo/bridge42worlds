Космическая вермишель: углеродная цепь в протопланетном диске

Cosmic Vermicelli: A Carbon Chain in a Protoplanetary Disk

Using the ALMA radio telescope, scientists detected the molecule HC5N—one of the longest carbon chains—in the disk around a young star. This shows that complex organic chemistry is possible even in cold regions where planets are born.
