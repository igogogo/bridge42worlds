Solar Magnetic Poles: First Shots from Beyond the Equator

The probe has measured the magnetic field at the Sun's poles for the first time. Previously, a flat view from Earth's orbit hindered observations. Now, climbing higher, it has shown that the north and south poles behave differently. This will help understand solar cycles.
