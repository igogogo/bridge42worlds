Hidden Gas Clumps Trick Astronomers in Early Galaxies

JWST studied three distant galaxies and found tiny, dense gas clumps dominate certain light signals. This makes standard methods underestimate elements like oxygen and nitrogen. New computer models that treat gas more realistically fix these errors, helping us understand galaxy formation.
