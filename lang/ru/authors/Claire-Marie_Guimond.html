Worlds with Eternal Magma Oceans

Scientists modeled the thermal evolution of sub-Neptunes and found that 98% of these worlds probably harbor a permanent magma ocean under their hydrogen atmosphere. This explains why the most common planet type in the galaxy stays hot inside, even though they were thought to have solidified long ago
