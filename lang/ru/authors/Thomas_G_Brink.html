Cosmic Heavyweight: Neutron Star Sets Mass Record

Astronomers measured the mass of a neutron star in a binary system—it turned out to be 2.35 times heavier than the Sun. This pushes the theoretical mass limit for such objects up to 2.27 solar masses. The result narrows down the possibilities for how matter behaves under unimaginable pressure.
